Privacy Policy for Piškvorky

Effective date: April 22, 2025

This Privacy Policy applies to version 2.0 and later of the Piškvorky mobile application.

The App has been redesigned to rely solely on Apple Game Center for multiplayer features, score tracking, and user identification.

1. What Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, or process any personal information or data directly from users.

All player-related functionality, including names, nicknames, achievements, and scores, is handled exclusively through Apple Game Center, in accordance with Apple’s own privacy policy.

2. How We Use the Data

As the developer, we do not access or use any personal data. All user-related data is managed entirely within Apple Game Center. We have no access to this information.

3. Data Storage and Retention

We do not store any user data on our own servers. Apple may store Game Center data in accordance with its own data policies, over which we have no control or access.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer any user data to third parties. Since no data is collected or stored, there is no data to share.

5. User Rights

As no personal data is collected or stored by us, there is no user data to manage or delete. For any data associated with Game Center, users should refer to Apple’s privacy settings and policies.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is designed to comply with Apple’s guidelines, including those related to children’s privacy. Apple’s Game Center includes its own parental controls and restrictions. We do not collect any information from children or any other users.

7. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes to the App or legal requirements. Any updates will be published at:

https://martin-mlatilik.github.io/piskvorky-public/privacy-policy/v2.html
